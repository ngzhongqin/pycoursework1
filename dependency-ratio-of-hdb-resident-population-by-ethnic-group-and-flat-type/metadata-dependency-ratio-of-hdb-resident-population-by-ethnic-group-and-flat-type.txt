Title: 'Sample Household Survey: Dependency Ratio of HDB Resident Population, by Ethnic
  Group and Flat Type'
Description:
  - 'Data is derived from the Sample Household Survey (SHS) which is conducted once
    every 5 years.'
  - ''
  - 'HDB resident population refers to Singapore citizens and Singapore permanent
    residents (SPRs) residing in HDB flats, excluding subtenants.'
  - ''
  - 'Data is based on per 100 population aged 15-64'
  - ''
  - 'The old-age dependency ratio is a measure of the relative size of the elderly
    resident population aged 65 years and above to that of the resident population
    aged between 15 years and 64 years.'
  - ''
  - 'The child dependency ratio is a measure of the relative size of non-working age
    resident population aged below 15 years to that of the resident population aged
    between 15 years and 64 years.'
